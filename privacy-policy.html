Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Stepup collects the following data to provide step counting functionality:

  • Motion and fitness data from your device's sensors
  • Basic device information for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Track and display your step count
  • Provide personalized feedback and notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

Your step data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal information on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Apple's HealthKit to access step counting functionality. This integration complies with Apple's privacy guidelines.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Control app permissions through your device settings
  • Delete app data by uninstalling the app

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
